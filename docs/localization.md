# Localization

The app is Amharic-first. This pass localized remaining visible English in the touched surfaces:

- Number page header/history tooltip
- Audio loading/unavailable/error text
- Settings title fallback
- Donate labels and copy feedback
- Bug report mailto flow
- Lyrics sheet/music controls

English proper nouns such as `PayPal`, `National Bank of Ethiopia`, URLs, and example email hints remain where they are names or user-input examples.
